Our Mission

Our goal is to create a strong and happy, multigenerational, owner-occupied urban cohousing community in the heart of Madison. Our community will thrive on collective decision-making, shared work, and shared resources, which will allow us to live sustainably, affordably, and in connection with each other.

Ownership

Our cohousing group advocates for home ownership to empower members with the opportunity to build equity and shape their own destinies, fostering a sense of ownership and financial stability while enabling individuals to actively participate in decisions that impact their lives and the collective community vision.

Sustainabiity

We aim to build for sustainability both in how we build our environment as well as targeting walkable neighborhoods. Not only to minimize our environmental impact but also to promote active lifestyles, enhance community cohesion, and foster a sense of interconnectedness among residents, leading to a healthier, more vibrant living environment both now and in the future.

Community

Our community stands as the nucleus of our built environment, where multigenerational bonds, strengthened by collaborative decision-making, shared labor, and pooled resources, create a vibrant and inclusive space that enriches the lives of all members.
